    Sticky Notes

    Sticky Notes is a simple Android app that lets users quickly create, edit, and save notes on their phone. It helps you remember important tasks, ideas, or reminders with colorful notes. All notes are saved on your device, so no internet is needed.

    Target Audience: Busy students, working professionals, and anyone who wants to quickly jot down ideas, reminders, or to-do lists without dealing with cluttered interfaces or login requirements.

The design focuses on simplicity, speed, and offline usability. Each note is saved with a random color for visual organization and optionally highlights the latest edited note for quick access or widget integration.

    Language Used: Kotlin, XML (for layouts)
